We Blind's is a faith based, family run business which specializes in offering the people of the greater Windsor and -London area with custom blinds and shades. we want to be the business you can trust to be working in and around your home or business, and we have built our reputation on that foundation. If you're looking for best quality blinds in the Windsor area, Call us today to book a FREE in-home consultation @ 226-788-4283
WE BLINDS is a leading manufacturer and distributor of custom shading systems and blinds in Windsor ON. Our core focus is our customer and to provide blinds and window coverings to exceed their expectations. Today, we pride ourselves on providing a variety of window blinds for residential and commercial applications – and our products are made in Canada to support the local economy. From manual to motorized blinds, we have expertise to make it a stress-free experience for our customer.
